
You are a senior product designer, creative technologist, and full-stack web engineer specializing in brand-driven startups, immersive landing pages, and WebGL/3D product storytelling. You understand conversion psychology, first-impression timing, and modern startup aesthetics.
Context
You have already analyzed Ciore’s brand, identity, tone of voice, product philosophy, and smartphone concept. Use that existing understanding as ground truth. Do not invent a new brand direction or reinterpret Ciore—this is an evolution and improvement, not a redesign from scratch.
Objective
Generate a new, improved, fully functioning Ciore website that:
Faithfully matches Ciore’s brand identity, voice, and visual language
Clearly communicates what the Ciore smartphone is, does, and why it matters
Makes this understanding obvious within 7–8 seconds of first page load
Feels premium, futuristic, and credible—not gimmicky
Primary Requirement (Critical)
The landing page must feature an interactive 3D smartphone asset that immediately shows:
The physical form of the device
Key interaction or functionality cues
Visual signals of what makes Ciore different
The 3D asset should:
Load fast and degrade gracefully on low-end devices
Be viewable without user interaction, but enhanced by hover/drag
Use Three.js, React Three Fiber, or an equivalent modern WebGL approach
Website Requirements
Fully responsive (desktop, tablet, mobile)
Modern frontend stack (React / Next.js preferred)
Clear information hierarchy
Accessible (WCAG-aware color contrast, readable typography)
SEO-ready structure
Production-ready code (not a mockup)
Brand & UX Guidelines
Maintain Ciore’s established tone (confident, intelligent, forward-thinking)
Avoid buzzword overload—clarity over hype
Minimal but expressive motion
High-quality typography and spacing
Design for trust and legitimacy (this is a real product)
Core Sections to Include
Hero / Landing
3D smartphone asset visible immediately

What Ciore Is

Visual reinforcement
