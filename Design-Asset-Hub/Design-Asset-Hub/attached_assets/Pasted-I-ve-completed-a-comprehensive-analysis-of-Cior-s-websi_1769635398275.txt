## **4. CONTENT HIERARCHY & USER EXPERIENCE ANALYSIS**

### **Strengths:**

1. **Clear Value Proposition:** Immediate understanding of Cioré's mission (sustainable, privacy-focused smartphones)

2. **Strong Visual Identity:** Consistent gold/green branding on black creates premium, tech-forward aesthetic

3. **Progressive Disclosure:** Accordion-style FAQs prevent information overload while keeping content accessible

4. **Multiple Conversion Paths:** Three distinct CTAs (Waitlist, Donate, Volunteer) cater to different user intentions

5. **Social Proof Integration:** Direct social media links in header and footer for credibility

6. **Transparency Message:** Explicit commitment to showing where donations go builds trust

### **Weaknesses & Opportunities for Improvement:**

1. **Single-Page Limitation:**
   - No separate About, Product Details, or Company Information pages
   - All information compressed into one scrolling experience
   - **Recommendation:** Activate dedicated pages for About, Product Specs, Progress/Roadmap, and Press

2. **Navigation Structure:**
   - Only "Home" in navigation menu
   - No quick-access navigation to key sections
   - **Recommendation:** Add sticky navigation with jump links (Features, Waitlist, Donate, Volunteer, Contact)

3. **Incomplete Information Architecture:**
   - 21 unpublished pages suggest incomplete site development
   - Duplicates indicate indecision on final structure
   - **Recommendation:** Consolidate duplicate pages, publish key pages (About, Progress, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions)

4. **External Form Dependency:**
   - All forms route to Google Forms (external experience)
   - Breaks brand continuity
   - **Recommendation:** Use Squarespace native forms or embedded Typeform for on-brand experience

5. **Limited Product Information:**
   - Features listed but no detailed specifications
   - No product imagery beyond abstract Earth visual
   - **Recommendation:** Add dedicated product showcase section with renders, specs, timeline

6. **No Clear Product Development Status:**
   - Users don't know if product is concept, prototype, or near-production
   - **Recommendation:** Add "Progress" or "Timeline" section showing development milestones

7. **Donation Section Placement:**
   - Donation functionality buried mid-page
   - Not prominent enough if fundraising is critical
   - **Recommendation:** Consider elevated placement or persistent donation CTA

8. **Footer Minimalism:**
   - No copyright info, privacy policy link, or terms & conditions
   - Missing standard legal/trust signals
   - **Recommendation:** Add footer links to Privacy Policy, Terms, Press, About pages

9. **Missing Analytics Mention:**
   - No visible privacy policy or cookie consent (GDPR/CCPA compliance concern)
   - **Recommendation:** Implement cookie consent banner, publish privacy policy

10. **Contact Form Generic Messaging:**
    - "We are currently revamping our website" message suggests incompleteness
    - **Recommendation:** Remove apologetic messaging once site is finalized
