Cioré is creating sustainable smartphones that protect your privacy and respect the planet.

Built with Intention.

At Cioré, Privacy First means your data stays yours. Our phones are designed with a custom operating system that does not track, listen, or monitor you in the background. Every feature is built to give you control, not take it away. We believe technology should protect your space, not invade it.

Our phones can draw energy from sunlight, indoor light, and even low light environments. This helps reduce electricity use and makes charging more flexible and sustainable. You can still plug in when needed, but every phone is built to work in harmony with renewable energy.

We use recycled and responsibly sourced materials in every part of the phone. Our packaging is plastic-free and our supply chain is designed to minimize waste and pollution while keeping our design clean and responsible.

Our phones are built in sections that easily come apart and fit back together. This saves money, reduces waste, and keeps your device lasting longer.

We are building artificial intelligence that respects the planet from the ground up. Our AI, called Gaia X, is designed to think responsibly, act sustainably, and protect your privacy. It is powered by solar and wind energy systems that reduce waste and pollution while keeping your information secure.

Cioré is a movement built by people who believe technology should serve everyone. Every phone we make is part of a bigger vision to create jobs, protect privacy, and give back to the communities that support us.
